International Secondary (Ages 13–18)
Core Activities
🦜

Release Decision

Use real vet data to debate and vote whether a live animal is ready for release. Vet team gives real-time feedback on your reasoning.

💰

Follow the Money

Map the illegal wildlife trade supply chain using real case data from southern Thailand. Find the weak links and propose solutions.

🌊

Blue Carbon Stock Market

Survey real mangrove trees, calculate carbon sequestration, and convert to Carbon Credits — linked to Verra and T-VER standards.

🤖

AI Wildlife Patrol

Train a Teachable Machine model on real camera trap images to distinguish wildlife, humans, and poachers. Build actual AI conservation tools.

IB · CAS · Service Learning

All activities generate documented outputs suitable for CAS portfolios, IB Extended Essay references, and school ESG reporting.

Learning Frameworks

Place-Based Education (PBE)

The Andaman coastline and WARF's active caseload are the curriculum. Every problem is real, local, and consequential.

Experiential Learning — Kolb's Cycle

Field data collection → Critical analysis → Systems thinking → Proposed interventions. Mirrors the professional conservation workflow.

What students take home
Real conservation science methodology applicable to future academic work
Understanding of global frameworks: carbon markets, wildlife law, AI in conservation
Documented project outputs for CAS / service learning portfolio
